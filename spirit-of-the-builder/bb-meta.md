## Abstract

Jschiarizzi reflects on the builder mindset and what it means to create inside RaidGuild and web3 communities. The session focuses on agency, craft, experimentation, collaboration, and the habits that help builders move from ideas to shipped work.

## Transcript Summary

Joseph tells a personal story of entering crypto through curiosity, learning Solidity quickly, and turning deep focus into opportunity. The session uses that story to explore the "spirit of the builder": curiosity, persistence, willingness to learn in public, and the ability to go deep enough on a problem that new paths open.

The talk is less about a specific tool and more about the habits that make technical growth possible. It encourages builders to take their own agency seriously, follow genuine interest, and treat intense learning and community participation as the foundation for long-term work in crypto.
