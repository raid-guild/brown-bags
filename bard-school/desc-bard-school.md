Bard School Demo Day presentation by Elco.

In this session, Elco introduces Bard School — a system designed to transform content creation into a repeatable, sustainable process for DAOs and communities.

Rather than relying on bursts of activity or ad-hoc marketing, Bard School proposes a minimal but consistent content strategy built around:

• Content buckets for collecting ideas and references  
• "Seeds" — long-form foundational content  
• A minimal monthly content calendar  
• Awareness funnels from discovery to commitment  
• Ritual-based workflows for sustainable production  

The system treats content not just as marketing, but as part of the coordination infrastructure of a DAO.

This demo explores how structured content workflows — supported by AI tools — can create long-term clarity, visibility, and growth for decentralized communities.

Key Concepts Covered:
• Content buckets and seed generation  
• Funnel awareness levels  
• Minimal content cadence strategies  
• AI-assisted content production  
• DAO marketing system design  

Bard School is part of the broader RaidGuild ecosystem of experiments in automation, communication, and community coordination.
