## Abstract

Dekan demos Daily Dust, an in-game, spatially aware newspaper for DUST. The walkthrough shows article creation, IPFS image selection, local discovery by player position, classified-style posts, curated front pages, and future uses such as lore, quests, walking tours, announcements, and world-based communication.

## Transcript Summary

Dekan walks through Daily Dust as a small but flexible publishing layer inside a game world. The demo starts with a player discovering a location, opening the Daily Dust mini app, drafting an article, attaching an IPFS-hosted image, selecting a category, previewing the post, and publishing it into the world.

The second part shows discovery modes: local articles based on spatial position, a back page for short posts and offers, general category search, a curated front page, and a pinned app that updates as the player moves. The future direction is broader than a newspaper: routes, quests, city tours, feature announcements, lore, and place-based instructions.
